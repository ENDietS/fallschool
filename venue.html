Venue & City

A place to learn, connect and explore.

The 2nd ENDietS Gather to Nourish School will take place alongside ICONSD2026 in Thessaloniki, Greece — a vibrant student city by the sea.

Conference venue

A.U.Th. Research Dissemination Center (KEDEA)

The ENDietS Gather to Nourish School will take place alongside ICONSD2026 at the A.U.Th. Research Dissemination Center (KEDEA), a modern venue located in the heart of Thessaloniki, within the Aristotle University campus.

Address 3is Septemvriou, Aristotle University Campus
546 36 Thessaloniki, Greece

How to reach KEDEA

Close to the city centre

KEDEA is located next to Thessaloniki city centre, on the Aristotle University campus, and is easily accessible by public transport.

  • Metro: “Panepistimio” stop
  • Bus: several bus lines stop within walking distance

About the city

Thessaloniki: a city to learn, connect and explore.

Thessaloniki is a vibrant city by the sea, known for its student atmosphere, history, culture, food and warm social life. As the 2026 Gather to Nourish School brings students and recent graduates together from across Europe, Thessaloniki offers the perfect setting to combine learning with connection and discovery.

Between sessions, participants may have the opportunity to explore the city, enjoy its waterfront, discover local food culture and experience the welcoming atmosphere of one of Greece’s most dynamic cities.

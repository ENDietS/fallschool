Registration Info

One registration, two experiences.

The 2nd ENDietS Gather to Nourish School will take place alongside the 3rd International Conference of Nutritional Sciences and Dietetics — ICONSD2026.

How registration works

ICONSD2026 + ENDietS Fall School

To participate in the ENDietS Fall School, participants must register through the official ICONSD2026 registration platform and select the registration option that includes the ENDietS Fall School.

This combined registration gives you access not only to ICONSD2026, but also to the student-focused programme organised by ENDietS, including ENDietS-led workshops and reduced-rate access to selected networking and social moments when an additional contribution is required.

Important note

Please select the correct registration option.

ICONSD2026 may offer separate registration options for participants who wish to attend the conference only.

Conference-only registration does not include access to the ENDietS Gather to Nourish School programme.

To join the Fall School, please make sure you select the option that explicitly includes the ENDietS Fall School.

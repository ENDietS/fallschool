ENDietS Team

Meet the team behind it all.

A volunteer-led European team of nutrition and dietetics students and recent graduates working to connect, support and represent students across Europe.

Who we are

Students and recent graduates, working across borders.

ENDietS is powered by students and recent graduates who volunteer their time to organise activities, create opportunities and strengthen the nutrition and dietetics student community across Europe.

Our team is made up of people who study, work, volunteer and collaborate across borders, bringing different perspectives, experiences and ideas to everything we do!

National Key Contacts

Connect with ENDietS in your own country.

Beyond the Executive Team, ENDietS is supported by National Key Contacts (NKCs) across Europe. NKCs help connect students with ENDietS, share opportunities in their countries and make it easier for students to reach us in their own language.

Explore our interactive NKC map to find the contact for your country and connect with ENDietS locally.

Welcome message

from ENDietS Presidents

A message to nutrition and dietetics students and recent graduates joining the 2nd ENDietS Gather to Nourish School.

Dear nutrition and dietetics students,

Following the success of the first edition of the ENDietS Gather to Nourish School, it became even clearer how valuable it is to create a space where students can come together, learn, connect, and grow beyond the classroom.

This second edition brings an even more special opportunity, as it will take place alongside the 3rd International Conference of Nutritional Sciences and Dietetics, organised by the Department of Nutritional Sciences and Dietetics of the International Hellenic University and the Hellenic Dietitians-Nutritionists Association.

Through this collaboration, students and recent graduates will have the chance to deepen their technical knowledge, engage with professionals, and take part in a high-quality scientific conference, while still experiencing the student connection, social moments, and European spirit that define ENDietS.

We hope these four days will be an inspiring opportunity to learn, connect, and nourish your personal and professional growth.

Welcome to the 2nd ENDietS Gather to Nourish School.

Ezgi Melody Kolay and Inês Teixeira Duarte
ENDietS Joint Presidents

Connecting nutrition and dietetics students across Europe.

ENDietS is part of EFAD, the European Federation of the Associations of Dietitians.

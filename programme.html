How the programme works

One Fall School, alongside one scientific conference.

The 2nd ENDietS Gather to Nourish School will take place alongside ICONSD2026, creating a unique opportunity for participants to combine a scientific conference experience with a student-focused Fall School programme.

The ENDietS programme will include a full student-centred day on Thursday, followed by selected ENDietS-led sessions on Friday and Saturday. Participants will also be able to attend ICONSD2026 as part of their registration.

The programme is currently being finalised and may be subject to changes. Final topics, speakers and timings will be confirmed closer to the event.
